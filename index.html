About Abarie.com

Born in Lagos and rooted in Nnewi, Anambra State — Okeke Ifeanyichukwu Daniel, the man the world knows as Abarie.com, didn't discover comedy. Comedy found him. From selling vegetables to fend for his large family, to working as a labourer at construction sites, his gift for making people laugh was simply undeniable. Abarie.com is also a professional MC, compere, public speaker, and online social media influencer.

His career ignited in 2021 and has not slowed since. Today he stands as one of Nigeria's sharpest stand-up voices and most in-demand comperes — a headline act who has shared stages with Nigeria's biggest comedy names. "From selling vegetables to fend for my large family, to being a labourer at construction sites — I never let my reality then judge my future." — Abarie.com
